About Office of Research

The Office of Research-NSU is the central office for research administration, functioning administratively in the Office of the Vice Chancellor. It is responsible for formulation of regulatory policies and procedures governing basic and applied research throughout the university and in facilitating and enhancing internal and extramural grant opportunities for core faculty. coordinates the activities of four School Scientific Review Committees (SRC). These SRC are responsible for scientific merit review of faculty research protocols that are part of grant applications. They also administers the process of ethics clearance undertaken by two university-wide committees: the Institutional Review Board/Ethics Review Committee and the Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC). The chairperson of each of these two university-wide committees reports to the Director

Dr. Swazo has held faculty appointment as Professor of Biomedical Ethics and Philosophy and administrative appointment as Interim/Acting Dean, College of Science and General Studies at Alfaisal University in Riyadh Saudi Arabia, as Professor of Philosophy and Chair of the Department of Philosophy and Humanities at the University of Alaska, Fairbanks, as well as professional experience as a Consultant with the Pan American Health Organization, Regional Office of the World Health Organization (PAHO/WHO), in the Regional Program on Appropriate Technology for Health and country-level in Honduras, Grenada, and Dominica. Professor Swazo specializes in ethics in international affairs, biomedical ethics, and philosophy of religion.
